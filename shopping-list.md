# Shopping list

Planning assumption: 14-15 confirmed so far, with room to scale toward 20-25 guests.

## Highest-impact buys

### Hosting infrastructure

- Name tags: "The event I thought this was: ______"
- Thick markers
- Pens
- Clipboards or a hard writing surface
- Painter's tape or masking tape
- Sticky notes
- Scorecards or index cards
- Small basket or bowl for votes
- Trash bags
- Paper towels
- Hand soap

### Food and drinks

- 6-8 snack types total, mixing salty, sweet, and fresh
- Chips and dips
- Popcorn or snack mix
- Veggie tray or fruit
- One easy substantial snack, like sliders, pizza bites, dumplings, or pigs in a blanket
- Birthday dessert or cake
- Ice: 2-3 bags
- Cups: 50-75
- Plates: 25-30
- Napkins: 100
- Drink labels or Sharpies
- Sparkling water or NA drinks
- Basic mixers if useful: soda water, tonic, ginger beer, citrus

### Prizes

- 1 "Best Wrong Event" prize
- 1 "Most Committed to the Bit" prize
- 1 "Most Confusing" prize
- 1 "I Wish I Were at That Event Too" prize
- 1 "Best Explanation" prize
- Cheap medals, ribbons, trophies, or sashes

### Decorations

The goal is intentional holiday/event whiplash.

- Balloons
- Streamers
- One birthday banner
- One random holiday banner
- Tablecloths in clashing holiday colors
- Fake flowers or wedding-ish decor
- Halloween cobwebs or pumpkins
- Valentine's hearts
- St Patrick's Day coins or shamrocks
- New Year's hats/noisemakers
- Christmas garland or bows
- A "registration desk" sign
- A photo wall or photo corner backdrop

### Game supplies

- Plastic Easter eggs: 40-60
- Candy, stickers, tiny toys, or prompts for eggs
- Mini wrapped gifts for White Elephant: 10-15, or ask guests to bring one optional silly item
- Playing cards
- Timer
- Small bells, noisemakers, or a whistle
- Blindfolds or scarves if doing any relay game
- Balloons for relay games
- Construction paper or cardstock

## Nice-to-have extras

- Disposable camera or instant camera
- Ring light or phone tripod
- Confetti poppers
- Costume emergency kit: safety pins, fashion tape, lint roller
- Temporary tattoos
- Tiny tiaras, party hats, or sunglasses for people who underdress
- "Wrong event" emergency accessories basket

## Last-minute store run list

- Ice
- Cups
- Napkins
- Extra snacks
- Extra drinks or mixers
- Trash bags
- Batteries if using lights or speakers
